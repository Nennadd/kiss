Deep Cleaning

We perform deep cleaning procedure that is by far the best way to treat your gums in case of a disease or inflammation. The purpose of this procedure is to get rid of dental calculus located in between the very tooth root and your gums causing them harm.

Esthetic Restorations

Esthetic restorations are aimed to fix faulty teeth, teeth that have been injured or restore missing teeth thus making them look flawlessly. Regardless of whether you need to have your teeth repaired or reshaped, this procedure will contribute to your healthy radiant smile that will sweep anyone off their feet.

Teeth Whitening

Our job is to make sure your teeth are healthy and pearly white. That’s why we pay close attention to offering you a wide range of white nuances. Our goal is to make your smile shine. Let’s not forget choosing the one that meets your needs contributes to skyrocketing self-confidence.

Crowns and Bridges

A missing tooth is no longer a nightmare! With our assistance, you can have a brand-new stunning ivory white tooth by attaching a dental bridge to imitate it. A tooth that has suffered trauma or has been severely damaged can be easily repaired by putting a crown. Make your smile perfect.

Full and Partial Dentures

If your tooth or part of it is missing, we will handle it with the utmost care. Our solution is using full and partial dentures that will adequately replace it. We can also provide help if a few teeth are missing. Give your bright smile a chance by trusting us!

Root Canals

If your tooth got infected, don’t worry! We will treat it by removing the infected part of a pulp tissue as well as the nerve after which we will cure the root canal. We will make sure it’s clean and shaped prior to filling it and adding a sealing substance.

Extractions

Many people are terrified of the mere thought of needing to have their teeth removed. Our experience, however, guarantees a minimum amount of pain and absolute dedication to your comfort and well-being. Unpleasant as it is, our team will ensure you are going to be taken care of properly.

Digital X-Rays

We provide a service such as digital X-rays. Its purpose is to enable us to get an insight into the overall condition of your teeth and establish if any medical treatment or procedure is required. This way we can detect a potential problem and alert you before it’s too late.

Implants

Implants provide steady support for replacement teeth that can be both removable and fixed. A durable basis is crucial as the replacement ones imitate your own teeth. Implants improve your self-esteem and dental health in the long run. Additionally, they positively affect your pronunciation if they are firmly attached.

Invisalign

Invisalign treatment is our recommended way of changing the position of teeth step by step. The main function of this treatment is to use a special mold that modifies teeth in such a way so as to place them in a perfect position. Moreover, it also has a corrective function.
