CROWNS AND BRIDGES

A missing tooth is no longer a nightmare! With our assistance, you can have a brand-new stunning ivory white tooth by attaching a dental crown or a bridge. Teeth that have been missing due to trauma can be easily replaced by placing a bridge in their original gaps.

ROOT CANALS

If your tooth got infected, don’t worry! We will treat it by removing the infected part of a pulp tissue as well as the nerve after which we will cure the root canal. We will make sure it’s clean and shaped before filling it and adding a sealing substance. Dental professionals at Kiss Dental will do their best save your tooth no matter how bad it might look.

IMPLANTS

Implants provide steady support for your teeth. Titanium-made implants are the best dental solution for replacement teeth either permanent or removable. A durable basis is crucial as the replacement ones imitate your own teeth. Implants improve your self-esteem and dental health in the long run.

INVISALIGN

Enjoy your brand-new smile Kiss Dental be proud to will provide you with! Take advantage of this most cutting-edge dental device! The invisible Invisalign treatment will reshape your teeth thus making your smile dazzling. Make use of utterly comfortable yet unstoppable teeth perfection reaching!
